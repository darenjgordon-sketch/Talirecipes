Halloumi & Sweet Chilli Wrap

  • 2–3 slices halloumi cheese
  • 1 tortilla wrap
  • Sweet chilli sauce
  • Optional greens (lettuce or spinach)

Method: Fry halloumi until golden. Place on the tortilla, drizzle with sweet chilli sauce and add greens. Roll up and enjoy — ready in under 10 minutes.
