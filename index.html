Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients: Pasta, tinned tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper.

Method: Cook pasta. Fry garlic in oil, add tomatoes, simmer, season, mix with pasta.

Veggie Stir Fry

Ingredients: Noodles, soy sauce, mixed veg, sesame oil.

Method: Cook noodles. Fry veg, add soy sauce, mix with noodles.
