Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread
  • ½ avocado
  • Pinch salt & pepper

Method

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Mash avocado on top.
  3. Sprinkle salt & pepper. ★ Use a fork to mash directly on toast
