Halloumi & Sweet Chilli Wrap

  • 2–3 slices halloumi cheese
  • 1 tortilla wrap
  • Sweet chilli sauce
  • Optional: lettuce or spinach

Method: Fry the halloumi slices in a non‑stick pan until golden. Place them on the tortilla wrap, drizzle generously with sweet chilli sauce, and add greens if you like. Roll up and enjoy—ready in under 10 minutes.
